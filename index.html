FAQ

Does this get you banned?

No, it only kicks users. We are working on a version that may include optional ban buttons.

Can I change the words?

Yes, and it is highly recommended to customize the word list based on your community's needs.

Does it work in private games?

Yes. P.U.R.E. works in both private and public games.

Can I whitelist users?

Yes, you can script that functionality yourself. A built-in whitelist system is planned.
